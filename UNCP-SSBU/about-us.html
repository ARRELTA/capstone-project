About the UNCPÂ Smash Hub

Originally, students at UNCP didn't have a place to gather together. Tournaments were by word-of-mouth, or by poster, and organizing for that was a pain.

Over the course of a semester, this website was created by students, for students, to hold tournaments, hold attention, and hold interest for a game that we all cherish, not to mention to ease the burden on organizers and tournament regulars alike when creating events.

All this culminated in what you're viewing now, and for that we sincerely thank you.
